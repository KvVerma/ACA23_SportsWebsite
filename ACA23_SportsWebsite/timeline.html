Timeline of Michael Phelps's Life

  • June 30, 1985 Michael Phelps was born.
  • Sixth Grade Phelps was diagnosed with ADHD (Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder)
  • Age of 10 Michael Phelps holds a national record for his age group.
    • Age of 15 Phelps qualifies for the 2000 Summer Olympics and becomes the youngest male to do so.
  • March 30, 2001 Michael Phelps breaks the world record in the 200-meter butterfly to become the youngest male ever to set a world record in swimming, at 15 years and 9 months.
  • 2012 Phelps retires from swimming for the first time.
  • April 2014 Phelps comes out of retirement and wins the 100-meter butterfly event at the Arena Grand Prix in Charlotte, North Carolina.
